Biography

    Chao Wu is currently an Associate Professor in Nanjing University of Science and Technology. He used to be a Post-doctoral researcher in Northeastern University under the supervision of Prof. Yanzhi Wang. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science with City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Chun Jason Xue. He has two master degrees, one in IC Design with HKUST and the other one in Detect Technology and Automation Instruments with Tongji University.

Research

    My research topics include AI Acceleration, Storage Architecture and File System.
